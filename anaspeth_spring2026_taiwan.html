Living in Taiwan

 

Out of all the places I've lived, I think that Taiwan was the family favorite! I lived there from around 2013 to 2016, or my 3rd Grade to 5th Grade. As a family, we visited so many different places and spent so much time together - I am forever grateful for all the bike rides we had together!

 

Taiwan is also where we first got Lola, our Bichon Frise... as of this year, she will be 14 years old!
