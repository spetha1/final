Seoul, South Korea

My mother is Korean, so when we still lived in Vietnam we would visit Seoul a lot in order to visit family and reconnect with a part of our cultural identity!

 

The last time we went back to Korea was in 2019. We've been to so many places within it as well, such as Gyeongju and Injie, Gangwon-do. Of course, my favorite parts were always the busking streets of Hongdae, but these days I find myself missing the national parks and autumn weather that Korea had. While I can't speak Korean, these trips to Korea are the closest I get to feel to my half Korean side of my heritage!
